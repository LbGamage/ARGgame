Summoning the Old God

Ingredients

  • Water drawn under the light of thirteen moons
  • Ghoul-fungus grown from the corpse of a willing host
  • Blood of seven untouched souls, taken willingly (or not)
  • Dried fingers of the freshly dead
  • Ground malachite from a sacred burial site
  • Bone fragments of forest-dwelling beasts (unblessed)
  • Hair from a drowned witch, retrieved before dawn
  • Candle wax made with graveyard tallow
  • A mirror darkened by eclipse shadow
  • The breath of a sleeping infant, captured in a black vial
