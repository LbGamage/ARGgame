Summoning the Old God

Ingredients

  • Water charged under the light of thirteen moons
  • Ghoul-fungus grown from the corpse of a human host
  • Blood of seven untouched souls, taken willingly (or not)
  • Dried wolvesbane
  • Ground malachite
  • Bone fragments of forest-dwelling beasts (unblessed)
  • Hair from a drowned witch, retrieved before dawn
  • Candle wax made with deer tallow
  • Newts eye from the lake

Instructions / Reflection

I can't go into the details of what we did, sorry stranger. It's on a need to know basis, I don't even know some of the steps. But, essentially, the coven attempted to summon the Old God of the forest.If you want to know more you'll have to join haha

We found traces of its presence during an outing to the cave systems south of the park. Well, not *we* exactly. Some members of the coven stumbled upon them almost twenty-four years ago. They've been preparing ever since. Be warned, I don't advice going there alone, especially if you're not familiar with the terrain.

On the night of the ritual, we bathed in the river beneath a waning crescent, lit sage, and carved protective sigils into the soil. The air was literally crackling with anticipation.

I was tasked with growing the ghoul fungus. It took time. But with the help of a few fae spells, I was able to lure someone in.

The ritual was performed on the night of samhain, it went on for hours. I think it worked, but we'll have to wait and see.
